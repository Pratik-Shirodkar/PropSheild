We have designed the architecture for a fully gasless experience to lower the barrier for non-crypto natives.

### 3. Private Real Estate Tokenization
Move beyond simple loans to **Tokenized Debt Positions**.
*   **Vision**: Allow lenders to buy fractional ownership of the debt (NFTs), enabling secondary market trading of these privacy-preserved assets.
